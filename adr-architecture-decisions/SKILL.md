# ADR Architecture Decisions

Use this skill as a basic starting point.

## Workflow

1. Review the technical decision that needs a durable record.
2. Identify context, decision, alternatives, and consequences.
3. Keep the ADR concise and easy to revisit later.
4. Focus on why the decision was made, not just what changed.
5. Verify the record is understandable without extra context.

## Output

- Produce practical architecture decision records.
- Keep technical reasoning explicit and reviewable.
- Prefer concise decision capture over long narrative documents.
