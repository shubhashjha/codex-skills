# Tenant Billing .NET

Use this skill as a basic starting point.

## Workflow

1. Review tenant boundaries, pricing rules, and billing triggers.
2. Identify billing events, usage capture, and invoice data requirements.
3. Keep financial calculations explicit and auditable.
4. Avoid mixing tenant resolution with billing calculation logic.
5. Verify representative billing scenarios and edge cases.

## Output

- Produce practical tenant billing changes.
- Keep billing rules explicit and reviewable.
- Prefer auditable calculation flows over hidden logic.
