# Node Cron Jobs

Use this skill as a basic starting point.

## Workflow

1. Review the scheduling requirements and operational constraints.
2. Identify frequency, failure handling, and monitoring needs.
3. Keep scheduled job logic isolated and testable.
4. Avoid overlapping executions where they are unsafe.
5. Verify schedule behavior and failure paths.

## Output

- Produce practical scheduled job changes.
- Keep automation reliable and easy to reason about.
- Prefer safe scheduling over aggressive job frequency.
