# Audit Trail Design

Use this skill as a basic starting point.

## Workflow

1. Review the business actions that require traceability.
2. Identify which events, fields, and actors must be recorded.
3. Keep audit records explicit, immutable, and queryable.
4. Avoid mixing operational logs with audit records.
5. Verify traceability for representative workflows.

## Output

- Produce practical audit trail improvements.
- Keep historical tracking reliable and maintainable.
- Prefer explicit event recording over vague logging.
