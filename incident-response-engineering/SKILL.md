# Incident Response Engineering

Use this skill as a basic starting point.

## Workflow

1. Review the incident symptoms, impact, and affected systems.
2. Identify immediate containment, diagnosis, and recovery priorities.
3. Keep actions ordered by risk and service restoration value.
4. Separate short-term mitigation from long-term remediation.
5. Verify recovery and capture follow-up actions clearly.

## Output

- Produce practical incident response guidance.
- Keep triage and recovery steps structured.
- Prefer clear operational response over vague escalation language.
